## üé® Style Consistency Tips

### Watercolor Tales (Zorro, Lumi, Luna)
- Use keywords: "watercolor", "hand-painted", "soft washes", "visible brush strokes"
- Emphasize: Paper texture, color bleeding, transparency
- Avoid: Sharp edges, digital effects, photorealism

### 3D Pixar Tale (Bruno)
- Use keywords: "3D Pixar-style", "volumetric lighting", "subsurface scattering", "photorealistic"
- Emphasize: Depth of field, fur physics, realistic rendering
- Avoid: Flat lighting, 2D effects, watercolor textures

### Soft Anime Tale (Nube)
- Use keywords: "soft anime", "large expressive eyes", "pastel colors", "simplified shapes"
- Emphasize: Rounded features, gentle shading, emotional expressions
- Avoid: Harsh lines, realistic details, complex backgrounds
